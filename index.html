Quick Disclaimer

Spring keyboards can be rather expensive as Unicomp sells the majority of their products for $100+. If you are interested in switching away from a membrane keyboard, I would suggest a mechanical keyboard with brown switches for general use, red switches for gaming, and blue switches if you want to be hated by those around you.
